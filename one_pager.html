Overview

Solo Station is a purpose-built, seller-facing POS terminal designed exclusively for restaurant and bar staff. It lives behind the counter, at the server station, or on the back bar where staff ring in orders, manage tabs, and close out checks. The 14" touchscreen gives servers and bartenders the screen real estate they need to navigate complex menus quickly during a rush. Built on the SQUID (Android) platform with integrated NFC and EMV payments, it replaces the need for a second screen entirely. Powered via USB-C through the included 60W adapter and accessories hub.
